About TeamsPlus

TeamsPlus is an upcoming browser extension designed to elevate your Microsoft Teams experience through advanced customization and productivity features. Currently in active development, the extension offers the following capabilities:

Core Features:

  • Tailored Visual Design: Adjust interface colors, fonts, font sizes, and element spacing to align with your preferences or organizational branding.
  • Granular Styling Controls: Fine-tune border radius, font weight, line height, and spacing to create a cohesive, visually optimized workspace.
  • Theme Ecosystem: Build, save, and export custom themes for personal use, or publish them to the TeamsPlus Theme Shop for community sharing. Browse and download themes created by other users to instantly refresh your Teams interface.

Additional enhancements are planned, including productivity tools, collaboration shortcuts, and deeper integration with Microsoft Teams workflows.
